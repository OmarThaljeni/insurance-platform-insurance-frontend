Medical Assistance to Crewmembers

Comprehensive medical support for injured crewmembers — from hospital follow-up to repatriation.

Medical Follow-up

Ongoing oversight of crewmember health at local hospitals and clinics throughout Tunisia.

  • Following up the health status of the patient at local hospitals and clinics
  • Striving to minimize medical costs as much as possible
  • Approving medical bills prior to settlement
  • Regular reporting to shipowners and P&I associations on the patient's condition

Repatriation of Crewmembers

Handling all formalities for the repatriation of deceased or severely injured crewmembers.

  • Arranging all administrative and logistical formalities
  • Ensuring repatriation of deceased crewmembers to their native countries
  • Coordinating with relevant consulates and local authorities
