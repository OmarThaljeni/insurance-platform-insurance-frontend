Handling Stowaways Matters

Expert handling of stowaway cases — from discovery to repatriation — across all Tunisian ports.

Discovery & Disembarkation

Assistance from the moment stowaways are discovered on board until formal disembarkation.

  • Assisting Master during investigations conducted by local authorities
  • Performing disembarkation and repatriation formalities to native countries
  • Coordinating with immigration and port authorities throughout the process

Evidence Collection

When local authorities refuse disembarkation, TIPIC ensures full documentation for the next port.

  • Collecting identity documents, photos and fingerprints
  • Forwarding all evidence to the P&I club and correspondent at the next port
  • Supporting possible disembarkation procedures at subsequent destinations
