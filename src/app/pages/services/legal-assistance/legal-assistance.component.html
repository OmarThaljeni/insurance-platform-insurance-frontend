Legal Assistance & Claims

Qualified jurists and appointed specialized lawyers handling all marine claims in Tunisian ports.

Cargo Claims Handling

In addition to cargo surveying, TIPIC handles cargo claims on behalf of shipowners and their P&I clubs.

  • Negotiating amicable settlements with local cargo interests
  • Defending claims before local courts through appointment of qualified lawyers
  • Recovery actions against third parties undertaken upon P&I club instructions

Vessel Arrest & Security

Rapid intervention when vessels are arrested in Tunisian ports.

  • Intervening to lift vessel arrest by issuing necessary and adequate security
  • Coordination with local authorities to minimize vessel downtime
  • Protecting shipowner interests throughout detention proceedings

FDD — Freight, Demurrage & Defence

Acting on behalf of shipowners and P&I associations to recover outstanding maritime dues.

  • Undertaking necessary actions with F.D.D. to collect unpaid freight and demurrages
  • Defending against unwarranted claims before Tunisian courts
  • Coordinating with international P&I clubs and legal correspondents
