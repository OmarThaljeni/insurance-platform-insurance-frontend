Pollution Cases

Rapid response to marine pollution incidents along the Tunisian coast and in all ports.

Incident Response

Immediate action to determine the nature, extent and cause of marine pollution events.

  • Determining the nature, extent and cause of pollution through qualified surveyors
  • Assisting Master during negotiations before local authorities
  • Signing necessary pollution reports and coordinating cleaning-up operations
  • Minimizing damage impact as much as practically possible

Master Detention Defence

In severe pollution cases, the Master may face pre-emptive detention pending investigation.

  • Immediate appointment of competent lawyer to defend Master's interests
  • Coordination with P&I club throughout the proceedings
  • Providing regular status updates to shipowners and their associations
